Problem statement: Accurate Grubhub Timing

College students who use delivery platforms like Grubhub often experience frustration due to unreliable time estimates, and inaccurate ready statues. This problem disrupts college students lives by inconveniently disrupting their time management. Therefore, to better serve the students busy lifestyle there is a need for a solution that can be more accurate and help student maintain their time management.
